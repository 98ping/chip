# writing-samples/

Drop your own writing here — the essays, papers, and posts you want Chip to sound
like. Plain text or markdown, one piece per file. Two or three is enough to start;
add more anytime to sharpen the match.

**These files are git-ignored on purpose.** Your real writing stays on your machine
and is never published. (Publishing your schoolwork can get your own work flagged by
plagiarism checkers later — not worth it. This README is the only thing in here that
gets committed.)

New here? Look in [`examples/`](../examples) for fictional sample essays and a finished
voice skill built from them, so you can see exactly what this looks like before adding
your own.

Once your samples are in place, ask Claude:
*"build my writing-voice skill from the samples in writing-samples/."*
